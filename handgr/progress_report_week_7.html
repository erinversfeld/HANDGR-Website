Progress

To date, the team has completed their reviews of the literature relevant to their respective devices in the context of South African Sign Language. They are now in the process of completing their project proposal and planning the way forward for the project as a whole.

Anna and Erin have both gained access to their devices, and Shaheel is in the process of obtaining his. Anna and Erin are both experimenting with collecting data from their devices. All team members are exploring the use of various machine learning libraries to implement the relevant algorithms.

Meeting minutes

A meeting was held on 17 May to resolve the following details of the project:

  • The number of algorithms to select for the study was settled upon.
  • All alphabet gestures are to be included in the study.
In addition to this, the content to be put in each section of the project propsal was discussed and agreed upon.

Additional work

  • Shaheel is to arrange access to the Kinect.
  • The group must finalise their draft of the project proposal.

Next milestone

The team is to begin their investigation of the various algorithms and data gathering methods to be used.
